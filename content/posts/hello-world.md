When I'm not on a computer, I love to ride my mountain bike, read a book, hike, teach and give presentations or have a good time with the people I love. We often hear about the cliché tech gal or guy who's a bit antisocial, but I would say I'm a balanced introvert: I need my time alone, but I'm also fuelled by meaningful social interactions.

My last and only experience with uploading content was through FTP so I was baffled by all the recent free tools to generate simple and fast static sites. Of course, I will detail that process in a future blog post.
